Get to Know Us

At Trot Dog Care, we’re more than just dog walkers — we’re a team of dedicated animal lovers who treat every pet like part of the family. Founded on a simple belief that dogs deserve the best, our mission is to provide exceptional care that’s consistent, personal, and filled with joy.

We know every dog has a unique personality, routine, and set of needs. That’s why we take time to build relationships not only with your furry friends, but also with you. Whether it's a walk, an overnight stay, or a grooming session, we approach every visit with love, patience, and professionalism.

Get to know the amazing humans who make this possible — each one carefully chosen for their experience, warmth, and genuine passion for pets. You can count on us to be where your dog feels at home, even when you’re not.
